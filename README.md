# Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project presents a **Blinkit Sales Analysis Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Excel** to analyze and visualize sales performance across various outlet types, item categories, and customer segments.  
The dashboard helps identify sales trends, performance drivers, and business insights that support **data-driven decision-making** and operational improvement.

---

## Dashboard Summary
| Metric | Value | Description |
|:---------------------------|:-----------:|:----------------------------------|
| **Total Sales** | $1.20M | Overall revenue generated |
| **Average Sales** | $141 | Average sales per item |
| **Number of Items** | 8,523 | Total distinct items sold |
| **Average Rating** | 4.0 | Customer satisfaction rating |

## Key Insights
- **Outlet Size:** Medium outlets contribute the highest sales ($507.9K, 42%), followed by high-size outlets ($444.8K, 37%).  
- **Outlet Location:** Tier 1 locations lead in sales ($472.1K), indicating strong urban demand.  
- **Item Type:** Fruits & Vegetables, Snack Foods, and Household items are top revenue generators.  
- **Fat Content:** Regular items account for 65% of total sales, highlighting product preference trends.  
- **Outlet Establishment:** Sales growth is steady from 2011 to 2018, with stabilization in later years.

---

## Dashboard Components
1. **KPI Indicators** – Displays total sales, average sales, total items, and ratings.  
2. **Sales Distribution Charts** – Breakdown by outlet size, item type, and location.  
3. **Trend Analysis** – Yearly outlet establishment chart for growth visualization.  
4. **Filter Panel** – Interactive filters for outlet size, location, and item type.  
5. **Segmented Insights** – Visual comparison of fat content and outlet performance.
